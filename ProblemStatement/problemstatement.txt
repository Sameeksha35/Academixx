 API Based Application

User Roles:
    • Admin: Has full access to manage the system, including creating, updating, and deleting students, teachers, and courses, and viewing all data.
    • Teacher: Can manage their own courses, enroll students in their courses, grade students, and view their enrolled students' details.
    • Student: Can view their enrolled courses, grades, and personal details.
Core Features:
    • User Management: Authentication and authorization for admin, teachers, and students.
    • Course Management: CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for courses.
    • Enrollment Management: Enroll students in courses.
    • Grading System: Teachers can assign grades to students.


An email should be triggered when a student gets registered and a weekly student progress report should be triggered via mail.
